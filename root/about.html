Viktoria Volokitina is a multidisciplinary designer and contemporary artist based in Abu Dhabi. Her practice spans branding, web3, product visuals, and storytelling. She is drawn to experiment, which is why this website was vibecoded over a few days.

I work in focused creative waves rather than on a fixed daily schedule, always with respect for deadlines. Each project is an immersive process, approached with care and intensity. My goal isn’t just to deliver, but to create something meaningful and lasting.
